Our Mission

Here at The Neat Shop, we understand how cluttered and busy life can get. Life happens, and we want you to be prepared and organized to handle it. We believe in the little things. That's why we're such avid supporters of journaling. Every little beautiful detail of life should be taken down in a book. We write down the things we don't want to forget.

Again, little things are important. Not spending 3 minutes untangling wires means that you get to listen to another song. A small dino night light will remind you of what it's like to be a kid again. Life is small.

Beginning to see what we're getting at here? Remember: life is simple. We are the ones who make it complicated. It's all about the eye contact and cups of tea. That's why our business model is also simple. No crazy flash sales or hassling with 5 different employees in a store. You order it, we'll ship it in 3 to 5 business days. Okay we'll admit we aren't perfect; if there is a mishap, just call our representatives and we'll get to your call as soon as possible.
